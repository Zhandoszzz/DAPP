Frequently Asked Questions

ChainChats is a decentralized chat application that allows users to communicate securely and privately. Utilizing blockchain technology, it ensures that conversations are encrypted and free from central oversight, providing a new level of privacy in digital communication.

ChainChats uses advanced encryption protocols within a decentralized framework. This means that messages are encrypted end-to-end, and only the communicating users have access to the content. There are no central servers where data is stored, significantly reducing the risk of data breaches or surveillance.

Yes, ChainChats is completely free to use. Our mission is to provide secure and private communication accessible to everyone, without the need for subscription fees or hidden costs.

Currently, ChainChats is designed to be used on a single device per account for security reasons. This ensures that your private keys, which secure your messages, are not exposed by being shared across multiple devices.

Unlike traditional chat applications, ChainChats operates on a decentralized network. This means there is no central point of control or data storage, giving users full control over their data and communications. Additionally, the decentralized nature of ChainChats makes it more resistant to censorship and external control.
